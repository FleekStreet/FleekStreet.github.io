Our Mission

We aim to fill this content gap by providing explanations that will allow you to understand complex topics and concepts with minimal mental gymnastics. From specific tools to the technologies they are built on, from topics in mathematics to coding shortcuts, from book reviews to project walkthroughs, and everything else in between, we aim to provide the most easily digestible technology material on the web.
